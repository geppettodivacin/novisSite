UTD's Premier A Cappella Ensemble

UT Dallas' premier a cappella ensemble, Novis is the leading definition of new direction: completely student-run and performing compositions by university students. They perform a variety of modern a cappella music at concerts and events, both on and off campus. Novis is currently directed by Julia Burroughs.
